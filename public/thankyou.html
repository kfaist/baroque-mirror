ARTIST STATEMENT

Baroque Mirror transforms the viewer into a living portrait, dissolving the boundary between observer and artwork. Through real-time AI transformation, your image becomes a brushstroke in an ever-evolving masterpiece—rendered in the dramatic chiaroscuro and opulent textures of the Old Masters.

This work explores identity, presence, and the democratization of portraiture. In an age where AI reshapes our relationship with images, Baroque Mirror invites you to see yourself as the aristocrats and saints of centuries past once saw themselves: immortalized in paint and light.

Every session is unique. Every portrait, ephemeral.
