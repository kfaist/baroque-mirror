About the Collection

This portrait system is built on an artist-crafted dataset drawn exclusively from public-domain sources. By using only open archives, the model absorbs visual languages rather than personal likenesses—learning motifs from Baroque and Rococo ornament, the humor and distortion of early caricature, and the confrontational spirit of French Revolutionary satire.

Archival Prints: Museum-quality giclée prints on Hahnemühle fine art paper, available in sizes from 8×10" to 30×40". Each print includes a certificate of authenticity signed by the artist.

NFT Editions: Mint your portraits as unique digital collectibles on the blockchain. Each NFT includes the high-resolution original, metadata about the transformation parameters, and perpetual exhibition rights.
